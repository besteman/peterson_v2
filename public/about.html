About

I wanted to create something that would provide people with a quote from Jordan Peterson per day. The reason was so that it would allow for a means for self-reflection and conversation. As I was building this, a quote appeared while I was testing. It said,

"When you have something to say, silence is a lie."

When I read that, it caused me to stop and take a scalpel to myself. I realized that I had things to say that I wasn't saying. Things that I should have said in my friendships, my marriage, and my work. I did not speak up, and I reasoned that it was better to keep quiet to stay safe and keep the peace. I was lying to myself and others. I was a coward.

At the end of the self-reflection, I knew why I was building this. I was making this so that people can have a chance to read a thought-provoking quote and use it as a springboard to help approve their lives.

I would like to make this site an ongoing project and add more features. Please considering supporting this project by using the links on the top right!
